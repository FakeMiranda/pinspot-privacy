Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Pin Spot app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Miguel Santos Miranda (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • Account Information: Email address used for authentication via AWS Cognito.
  • Purchase History: Transaction identifiers provided by Google Play Billing for premium features.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent in the app, and crash diagnostics.
  • Device IDs: For push notifications and advertising purposes.

Data Deletion & Account Closure

The Service Provider provides a transparent way for users to request the deletion of their data, in compliance with Google Play's Data Safety policies:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the Application by navigating to the Profile settings and selecting "Delete Account".
  • In-App Deletion ( Single spot items ): You can delete the pin spot items individually without deleting the whole account from the spot item selection
  • Manual Request: If you are unable to access the Application, you may request that your account and all associated data be deleted by emailing us at mouxum.inc@gmail.com.
